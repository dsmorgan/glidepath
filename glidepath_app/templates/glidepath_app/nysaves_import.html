2. Use it

  1. Log into NYSaves and open an account's holdings / summary view.
  2. Click the bookmarklet. It reads the account number, beneficiary, and per-portfolio units.
  3. Send imports directly; Copy as CSV puts it on your clipboard to upload on the Accounts page.
  4. Have more than one beneficiary? Switch accounts in the NYSaves dropdown and run it again.

Note: a browser won't let an HTTPS page (NYSaves) POST to http://localhost (mixed content). For a local Glidepath, use Copy as CSV; the direct Send is for your deployed HTTPS site.
